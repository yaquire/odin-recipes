Recipe

  1. Boil the Water (I recommend boiling the water first)
  2. Put the noodles in the boiling water
  3. Drain the water from the cooked noodles
  4. Mix the powder with the bioling water
  5. Add the mixture to the noodles & boil, add water as needed (water level at least reaches the noodle level)
  6. Add other items if needed & Enjoy :)
